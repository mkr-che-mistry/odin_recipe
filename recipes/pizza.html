Pizza Night! Craft Your Own Masterpiece

Ingredients (for one 12-inch pizza)

  • All-purpose flour: 1 ½ cups
  • Active dry yeast: 1 teaspoon
  • Warm water: ¾ cup (adjust for dough consistency)
  • Olive oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Pizza sauce: ½ cup (your favorite brand or homemade)
  • Mozzarella cheese: ½ cup, shredded
  • Pizza toppings (of your choice): pepperoni, sausage, vegetables, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes until the yeast is foamy and activated.
  2. Add olive oil and salt to the yeast mixture. Gradually stir in flour until a soft dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for 5-7 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
    4. **Preheat your oven to its highest temperature setting (ideally 475°F or higher) and preheat a baking sheet or pizza stone along with the oven for at least 15 minutes.**
  4. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a lightly floured surface into a 12-inch circle. Transfer the dough to your preheated baking sheet or pizza stone.
  5. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edge. Top with your favorite pizza toppings and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your homemade creation!
