The Reuben Sandwich: A Hearty and Flavorful Treat

Ingredients

  • Rye bread: 2 slices
  • Corned beef: thin-sliced, enough to cover one slice of bread
  • Sauerkraut: drained, about Â¼ cup
  • Swiss cheese: 1 slice
  • Thousand Island dressing: 1 tablespoon
  • Butter: 1 tablespoon, softened

Instructions

  1. Spread butter evenly over one side of each bread slice.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Place one slice of buttered bread, butter-side down, in the skillet.
  3. Top the bread with sauerkraut, then corned beef, and finally the Swiss cheese slice.
  4. Spread Thousand Island dressing on the other slice of buttered bread and place it on top, butter-side up, creating a sandwich.
  5. Cook the sandwich for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and the cheese is melted. Gently press down on the sandwich with a spatula while cooking to ensure even browning.
  6. Carefully transfer the sandwich to a plate and cut it in half diagonally. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Reuben!
